# yolo Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 2025**

yolo is built with your privacy at its core.

## What We Collect

Nothing. yolo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Microphone Access

yolo uses your microphone solely to detect movement sounds during your wake window. Audio is processed on your device in real-time. Nothing is ever recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

## Data Storage

Your settings are stored locally on your device only. Nothing leaves your phone.

## Third Parties

yolo uses no analytics, tracking, or advertising.

## Contact

Questions? Contact: support@yoloalarm.app
