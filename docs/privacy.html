Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

yolo alarm is built with your privacy at its core.

What We Collect

Nothing. yolo alarm does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Microphone Access

yolo alarm uses your microphone solely to detect movement sounds during your wake window. Audio is processed on your device in real-time. Nothing is ever recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

Data Storage

Your settings are stored locally on your device only. Nothing leaves your phone.

Third Parties

yolo alarm uses no analytics, tracking, or advertising.
