About

yolo alarm wakes you gently when your body is ready. Set a wake window, and the app listens for the subtle sounds of you naturally stirring to wake you at the perfect moment.

How It Works

  • Set your earliest and latest wake times
  • Tap Start and drift off to sleep
  • When yolo detects you stirring, it gently wakes you
  • Start your day feeling ready, not groggy

Privacy

Your privacy matters. yolo alarm processes all audio on-device in real-time. Nothing is ever recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. Read our full privacy policy.

Support

Having issues or questions? We're here to help.

  • Make sure microphone permissions are enabled
  • Try adjusting the sensitivity slider in settings
  • Ensure your device isn't in silent mode for alarm sounds
